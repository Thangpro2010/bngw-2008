The Orchid Flower, Its Meanings and Symbolism

Orchids are the largest family of blooming flowers with over 25,000 species and over 100,000 varieties. They are often are tropical beauties. Wild orchids grow worlwide and can be found on every continent except Antarctia. These flower have earned the reputation as difficult to grow, probably due to their need for filtered light and high relative humidity. Many varieties, such as the moth orchid (Phalaenopsis) are surprisingly easy to grow as a houseplant


What Does the Orchid Flower Mean?


The orchid has been held in high regard since ancient times. It symbolizes

  • Love
  • Beauty
  • Fertility
  • Refinement
  • Thoughtfulness
  • Charm
